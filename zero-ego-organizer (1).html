A new way to show up for collective work

Show up.
Or say so.

The only rule: your absence is data. give it.
What this is
Not about guilt. About logistics. Your empty chair is a variable no one budgeted for.
Not about ego. Yours or mine. No drama. Just signal vs. silence.
Not a performance. You don't have to be fine. You have to be findable.
The actual protocol
Coming? Nothing required. See you there.
Not coming? One message. "Can't make it." Done. That's the whole thing.
Monitoring a channel? Be honest about it. Silence is also an answer — a bad one.
Burning out? Say that too. We'll figure it out. We can't plan around a ghost.
"Taking care of yourself is valid. Making it invisible is where the group pays the price."
